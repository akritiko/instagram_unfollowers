Instagram Unfollowers Finder

Instructions
  1. Download your information from your Instagram Account Center.
  2. Choose Followers and Following information for all time and .html format.
  3. Once the download is ready, Instagram will inform you with an e-mail.
  4. Download the data (zip format) and unzip it.
  5. Go to the unzipped folder and check for "following.html" and "followers_1.html" files.
  6. Upload both files below.
Data security: Please note that this application does not keep any of the files you share with it on our servers. This includes both followers and following input files and the resulting output file with the unfollowers.
